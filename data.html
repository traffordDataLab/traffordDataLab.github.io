Data

We are committed to the sourcing, preparation and publication of open data. We aim to ensure that all the open data we publish has a minimum 3-star openness rating, is accessible on the Web in a machine readable non-proprietary format and is covered by the OGL v3.0 licence which permits anyone to reuse it. We store our datasets in our dedicated open data repository on GitHub.

Choose from the following datasets to open a page containing a preview, download links and further information, e.g. date of publication, source etc. (metadata).

Data

We are committed to the sourcing, preparation and publication of open data. We aim to ensure that all the open data we publish has a minimum 3-star openness rating, is accessible on the Web in a machine readable non-proprietary format and with a licence that permits anyone to reuse it. We store our datasets in our dedicated open_data repository on GitHub.

Select the following datasets to open a dedicated page in a new window containing the metadata (date of publication, source etc.), a preview and links to download the chosen dataset.

The Trafford Data Lab supports decision-making in Trafford, Greater Manchester, by revealing patterns in data through visualisation. You can find out more about us here. We provide various outputs from raw open data to visualisations as thematic profiles and interactive maps. We are also involved in various open data and data visualisation projects.

News

Below you can find links to recent publications by the Lab including instructive blog posts and data releases.

February 2019

  • Info: Launch of our new Ward Profiler application
    The app visualises over 70 different ward level indicators in a map and linked lollipop chart.

January 2019

  • Info: Publication of our Interactive Graphics Companion
    The Companion provides the code to create different interactive data visualisations in Vega-Lite.

December 2018

  • We published daily digital literacy tips for colleagues at Trafford Council during the run up to Christmas.

November 2018

  • Info: Launch of our interactive Crime Scanner application.
    Interactively visualise street level crime and anti-social behaviour (ASB) incidents in your area using data from data.police.uk. View and export data for the last 3 years for the whole of England at Local Authority level through to Electoral Ward and LSOA where available, filtered by category.
  • Tool: Leaflet accepts our Reachability plugin.
    Following the release in September, the Leaflet mapping library have included our reachability plugin in their list of approved plugins.

October 2018

  • Project: Release of our OpenGovIntelligence applications.
    A suite of three web applications have been developed as part of the OpenGovIntelligence project. The apps with accompanying documentation can be found on our dedicated project landing page. We have also published a Medium article on improving the discoverability of worklessness data to accompany this release.
  • Map: Updated road casualties data interactive application.
    We have released an update to our web application that allows users to explore road casualties reported in Trafford during 2017.

September 2018

  • Map: Release of our new reachability plugin for our Explore mapping application.
    Read more about it in our Medium article.
